About Chefle

Chefle is an independent daily food guessing game. Each day, players narrow down a mystery dish by comparing familiar food clues such as region, protein, base carb, serving temperature, calories, texture, and prep time.

What Makes The Game Original

The puzzle logic, daily rotation, scoring, share grid, filters, accessibility behavior, and dish comparison system are built specifically for Chefle. The game is designed for casual play, not recipe memorization.

Dish Archive And Editorial Method

The archive is a curated set of recognizable global dishes. Dish categories and numeric values are simplified editorial estimates based on common preparations so the puzzle stays readable and consistent.

  • Regions describe broad culinary geography, not one exact recipe origin.
  • Calories and prep times are rounded estimates for gameplay.
  • Photos are selected to visually represent the dish archetype.

Independence

Chefle is not affiliated with HelloFresh, Allrecipes, Google, Squarespace, or any restaurant, grocery, recipe publisher, or meal-kit brand. Brand and recipe references, when used, are descriptive context only.
