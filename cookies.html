Chefle Cookie Notice

The Chefle app code does not set cookies and does not include login cookies or payment cookies.

Local Storage

Chefle uses browser local storage, not cookies, to remember game progress, stats, settings, and trusted-time information on your device.

Hosting And Squarespace

If Chefle is hosted or embedded through Squarespace, Squarespace and the static host may set or read their own cookies and process normal technical data according to their own policies and settings.

Advertising Cookies

If Google AdSense or another ad service is enabled on the surrounding website, advertising cookies or similar identifiers may be used for ad delivery, fraud prevention, measurement, frequency capping, and personalization where allowed.

Clearing Data

You can clear Chefle local storage and any related site cookies through your browser's site data controls.
