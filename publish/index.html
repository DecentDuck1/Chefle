      How to Play
      
        
      
        
      
      
      
      
        
      
          
      
            
      Goal
      
            
      Guess the hidden Chefle dish. Today’s region group is revealed up front, and the pool on the right only shows dishes from that group. The answer is always in that pool.
      
          
      

          
      
            
      Making a Guess
      
            
      Use the recipe search or the pool filters for protein and base carb. Clicking a pool item fills the search box; press Guess, press Enter on an exact dish name, or choose a search suggestion to submit.
      
            
      You have 7 official guesses. If you miss all 7, the daily game is marked as a loss, then you can reveal the answer or keep guessing afterward for practice.
      
          
      

          
      
            
      Reading the Row
      
            
      The dish name on the left is only the name you guessed. The scored tiles compare protein, base carb, temperature, calories, texture, and prep/cook time.
      
            
        
              
      • Sage with a check means that field matches the answer.
        • 
              
      • Terracotta means that field is different.
        • 
              
      • For calories and prep/cook time, an up arrow means the answer is higher than your guess. A down arrow means the answer is lower.
        • 
            
      
          
      

          
      
            
      Categories
      
            
      Temperature is one of Cold, Room Temperature, or Hot. Texture uses broad groups such as Creamy, Crispy, Soft, and Tender. Base carb uses grouped categories such as Bread & Wraps, Pasta & Noodles, Rice & Grains, Potatoes, and No Starch.
      
          
      

          
      
            
      Infinite Mode
      
            
      Use the ∞ button beside help to switch modes. Infinite mode gives you a fresh hidden dish after every correct answer or failed 7-guess round; correct answers raise your streak, and a failed round resets it. Your best streak is saved on this device.
      
          
      

          
      
            
      Stats and Sharing
      
            
      Stats use the official 7-guess result: played games, win percentage, streaks, a 1-7 guess distribution, and failed games. The share summary includes the game link and emoji rows for the official guesses.
